History

Basavanbagewadi, located in the Vijayapura district of Karnataka, is known for its historical significance and is the birthplace of the social reformer Basavanna, the founder of the Lingayat faith.

Places to Visit

  • Basaveshwara Temple

Local Culture

The town celebrates festivals like Basava Jayanti with great enthusiasm. The culture here is deeply influenced by Lingayat traditions and Kannada heritage.
